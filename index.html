Sergi Quintana

Postdoctoral Researcher

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at ROCKWOOL Foundation Berlin and a Research Associate at the Berlin School of Economics. I hold a PhD in Economics from the IDEA Graduate Program at the Universitat Autonòma de Barcelona (UAB) and Barcelona School of Economics.

My research fields are in Applied Microeconomics, Human Capital, Economics of Education and Labor Economics.

In my research, I combine structural models with RCTs and reduced-form methods to study human capital formation and investment throughout the life cycle.
